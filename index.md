### Question 1: 

Evaluate the following limits: 

(i) 
$$\lim_{({x\rightarrow 0},
{y\rightarrow 0})} \frac{x^2}{x^4+y^2}$$

(ii)
$$\lim_{({x\rightarrow 0},
{y\rightarrow 0})} \frac{xy}{y^2-x^2}$$

(iii)
$$\lim_{({x\rightarrow \infty},
{y\rightarrow 3})} \frac {2xy-3}{x^3+4y^3}$$

(iv)
$$\lim_{({x\rightarrow 1},
{y\rightarrow 1})} \frac{x(y-1)}{y(x-1)}$$
